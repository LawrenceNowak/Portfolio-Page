Time Tracker Webapp

In order to increase my own productivity as well as tracking my own work pace, I used (and am still using) a webapp called Clockify.me. Its a free online time tracker that you use in your browser. The only issue I had was that I am using the pomodoro technique. In short: The pomodoro technique is a method to increase ones productivity by splitting up a taskt in so called pomodori, which lasts 25 minutes. After one pomodori a 5 minute break ist held, after 4 pomodori, i.e. the 4th break, a longer break is being conducted, which lasts from 20 minutes to 25 minutes. The pomodori technique ensures high focus on any given task by eliminating the risk of procrastination. It gives you little timechunks where you can do whatever you please and scratch your procrastination itch. With the app I am currently working on I want to combine a timetracker with the pomodori technique to extract the net working time and the total working time. Also I want to visualise my data in form of graphs and whatnot
